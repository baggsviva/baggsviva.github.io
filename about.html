About Me

By the way, I'm Gabb Vivares. I had been working as a booking salesman for a distributing company that serves fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) across the region for almost 6 years. During that time, I also pursued web development as a sideline starting in 2018. However, when the pandemic hit and persisted for nearly four (4) consecutive years, I, like many others, made the decision to fully focus on online work as a web developer. This shift allowed me to prioritize safety and spend valuable time with my family. Now, I'm venturing into a new chapter of my career as a web developer, seeking fresh opportunities in the online job market. This marks an exciting new beginning for my professional journey.

I am hoping that there is an opportunity for me to help you and your business. There is no perfect in this world and I'm far from perfect but I assure you that I am sincere, honest, functional and with a great attitude; qualities that every employer wants to aim for.
