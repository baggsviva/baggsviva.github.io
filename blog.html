Captured Moments Before Departing for CDO...

After a restful and reflective Holy Week spent on a long weekend in the beautiful Island of Camiguin, it is now time to return to our home base in Cagayan de Oro City, famously known as the 'City of Golden Friendship'.

Eid'l fitr 2023

El Salvador Outing, Apr 21, 2023

Eid'l Fitr - Speargun Fishing...

Eid'l Fitr is one of the most enjoyable holidays in the Philippines, as our Muslim brothers and sisters celebrate their annual festivity. In contrast, as Christians, we also spend quality time with our families, whether it's going to the beach or anywhere else where we can create enjoyable moments with our loved ones.

That's why the team "baggsviva" headed to El Salvador, a nearby town in Cagayan de Oro City. It boasts free public beaches where you can take a dip, and the locals are known for their hospitality. We decided to try out our newly bought spearfishing gun. Unfortunately, the shoreline we visited had a limited variety of marine life. It was quite empty, with scarce sea shells, eels, sea urchins, crabs, and so on. Nonetheless, the kids had a great time and thoroughly enjoyed the adventure on that day.

River Boulevard

Just Walking, May 28, 2023

At the moment prior to the opening of River Boulevard for vehicle transportation.

This evening, we took advantage of the opportunity to enjoy a free public walk on the highway. The local government provided this chance for citizens to witness the beautiful project by JICA in CDO. Many people engaged in various physical activities such as walking, jogging, skateboarding, and cycling. We had a great time and prioritized our physical well-being through these activities. However, during our long walk, our elder daughter, Johanne, started experiencing a headache. We decided to head back home, and unfortunately, she vomited along the road-side while we were driving from the boulevard to our house. As a result, she won't be able to attend school the following day as she needs time to rest and recover.
